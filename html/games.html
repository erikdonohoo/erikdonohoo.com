Mario

Who doesn't love Mario! Try my version of the original. Mario runs all by himself but you need to time your jumps and ducks carefully. Run and jump on #2 pencils to get those hard to reach coins and baddies. Your score is based on how many coins you collect and enemies you kill. Stay alive as long as you can and see if you can make it into the top scores!
