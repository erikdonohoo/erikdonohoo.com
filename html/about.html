What is an erikdonohoo.com account?

With your erikdonohoo.com account you can track your scores on any of the games here that I have made. You don't need an account to play, but isn't it more fun to see how bad you are destroying the competition? With an account, you can compete against others, view scoreboards, and try to work your way up the ranks. It's totally free and you only need to choose a username and password and link with an email account. The email is used ONLY for account resets and for linking to gravatar. If you would like a custom avatar, make sure you check 'use gravatar' when you register. You can always go back and update it later. If you do choose to use gravatar, make sure you use the same email here that you use for the gravatar service.
