What is an erikdonohoo.com account?

With your erikdonohoo.com account you can play any of the games here that I have made. You can compete against others, view scoreboards, and try to work your way up the ranks. It's totally free and you only need to create a username and password. If you would like a custom avatar, make sure you check 'use gravatar' when you register. You can always go back and update it later. If you do choose to use gravatar, you will need to add an email address. It is only used to pull your gravatar avatar down from the cloud.
